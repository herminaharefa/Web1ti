What is UNAI?

Universitas Advent Indonesia(UNAI) is a Higher Education institution managed by the Seventh-day Andventist Church of the Western Union Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was

originally called Opleiding School der Andvent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Seventh-day Andventist Church

Universitas Andvent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

1)Faculty of Philosophy
2)Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
3)Faculty of Economics
4)Faculty of Nursing
5)Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
6)Faculty of Information Technology
